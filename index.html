I am a Wellcome Trust funded Bioinformatics and Theoretical Systems Biology PhD candidate in the department of Metabolism, Digestion and Reproduction. My research focuses on the development of methods for the interpretation of high-dimensional omics datasets and the use of a priori biological knowledge to assist this. More specific research interests include pathway analysis, multi-omics data integration, and predictive modelling for phenotype classification and biomarker discovery.
